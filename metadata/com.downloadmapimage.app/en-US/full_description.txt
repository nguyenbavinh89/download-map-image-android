Download Google Map Image is a simple WebView-based companion app for
downloadmapimage.com, a free tool that lets you download Google Maps
images and Street View 360\xc2\xb0 panoramas without needing to log in or use
an API key.

Features:
* Download static map images directly from a Google Maps URL or place
* Download Street View 360\xc2\xb0 panoramas
* Share a Google Maps link from another app directly into this app
* No login or account required
* No API key required

This app loads downloadmapimage.com inside a WebView to fetch and
process the images, so an internet connection to that service is
required for the app to work.
